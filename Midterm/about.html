About The Author

Kamani Millington is a Junior, Emerging Media at Hunter College. He's always been interested in the history of New York's Infrastructure, and how our city was built up through it.

With years of experience utilizing researching the transportation in the city, it has become a dream of mine to create a resource for people with similar interests, to easily find photographic research regarding this. In addition to knowledge, photos and illustrations are a passion of mine, and this seems to be a perfect place to show them.
