Midterm Ideas 



As someone very interested in the photographic history of New York City, I’d love a well-designed resource to organize and view more obscure aspects of New York’s history. While there are a few sites like this, a lot are with outdated visuals or are disorganized. I would like to incorporate my design skills, research, and photography into this website to create an easy-to-use resource. This will also serve as a portfolio, promoting my project to take “before and after” photos of local, more unknown locations in New York City.

My Second site Idea is a portfolio website that is inspired by Online Art Galleries.As I am getting into the field, I must have a resource to display my work. While I could use those online site templates, having a site made by myself would be even better from both a design standpoint, and show experience.
